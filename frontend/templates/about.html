About MedGuard

MedGuard is a comprehensive web application designed with a singular, critical mission: to detect, report, and manage counterfeit drugs, thereby safeguarding public health across Nigeria. It serves as a vital bridge between the public and regulatory bodies like NAFDAC, empowering every citizen to become a guardian of our nation's medicine supply.

The Problems We Solve

The circulation of counterfeit drugs is a silent epidemic with devastating consequences. MedGuard was built to directly address these critical challenges:

  • Protecting Lives: Fake medications can contain incorrect ingredients, wrong dosages, or even toxic substances, leading to treatment failure, severe health complications, and death. MedGuard provides a first line of defense.
  • Building Trust: The fear of counterfeit drugs erodes public trust in our healthcare system. By providing a reliable verification tool, we help restore confidence in pharmacies and medical providers.
  • Bridging the Information Gap: Many citizens lack an easy, immediate way to verify their medications. MedGuard closes this gap with accessible technology, including offline SMS functionality for those without internet access.
  • Empowering Regulators: By crowdsourcing real-time data on suspicious products, MedGuard provides authorities like NAFDAC with the actionable intelligence needed to conduct targeted investigations and protect the public more effectively.

Our Features

  • Instant QR Code & Batch Verification: Instantly validate a drug's authenticity against a secure, regulator-managed database.
  • Anonymous Counterfeit Reporting: Easily submit reports of suspicious medications with photographic evidence to alert authorities.
  • Admin Dashboard for Regulators: A powerful dashboard for authorities to view, filter, and manage all submitted reports in real-time.
  • Smart Scan Technology: The system can intelligently analyze any QR code to provide helpful information, even for unregistered drugs.
