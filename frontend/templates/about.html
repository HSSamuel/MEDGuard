About MedGuard

MedGuard is a comprehensive web application designed with a singular, critical mission: to detect, report, and manage counterfeit drugs, thereby safeguarding public health across Nigeria. It serves as a vital bridge between the public and regulatory bodies like NAFDAC, empowering every citizen to become a guardian of our nation's medicine supply.

Our Features

  • Instant QR Code Verification: Scan a drug's packaging to instantly validate its authenticity against our secure, central database.
  • Anonymous Counterfeit Reporting: Easily submit reports of suspicious medications, including batch numbers, locations, and photographic evidence to alert authorities.
  • Admin Dashboard for Regulators: A powerful dashboard for authorities to view, filter, and manage all submitted reports in real-time.
  • SMS & Offline Functionality: Verify drugs even without an internet connection, ensuring accessibility for all Nigerians, regardless of their location or device.

Our Technology

MedGuard is built on a reliable and scalable technology stack, featuring a Python Flask backend and a dynamic HTML, CSS, and JavaScript frontend. This modern architecture ensures the platform is both robust for regulators and easy to use for the general public.
